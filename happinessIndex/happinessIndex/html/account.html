Operation Happiness

Welcome to your Employee Happiness Detection Software

This software allows for you to manage the overall health of your employees. You can see their overall sentiment score on a scale of 100.
If the employee ever has their score drop more than 20 points within a day, they will be prompted to respond to a survey to see what affected their experience that day.

This allows for actionable management and feedback on the days that are especially difficult for your employees.
